The framework ships with five general agents (Lab Director, Quantitative Scientist, Science Writer, Literature Extractor, Extraction Validator). Phase 5 adds domain specialists.

Examples: "camera-trap wildlife specialist", "small-mammal population ecologist", "vegetation ecologist", "movement ecologist", "physical oceanographer", "behavioural neuroscientist".
